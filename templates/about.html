About ESL Learning Hub

My name is Jehyeok Yeon, and I am currently a sophomore at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, studying Computer Science and Linguistics.

The ESL Learning Hub is born out of my passion to provide a more interactive and accessible way for ESL students to learn English. In an era where private tutoring can be costly and sometimes less effective than desired, I envisioned a platform that harnesses the power of AI to enhance language learning experiences.

Our mission is to empower ESL learners by offering a user-friendly web app that not only checks grammar but also provides real-time feedback, explanations, and examples to make the learning process engaging and effective.

Meet Our Team

The ESL Learning Hub is a solo project, developed and maintained by Jehyeok Yeon, combining expertise in both computer science and linguistics to create an innovative learning platform for ESL students.
